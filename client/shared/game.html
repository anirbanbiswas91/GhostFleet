    How to Play

    Set up: Pick a ship from Fleet Dock, choose its direction, then tap your board to place it.

    Adjust: Tap a placed ship to edit it. Drag it to valid cells or rotate it. Tap empty water to leave edit mode.

    Quick setup: Auto-Place rerolls your whole fleet every time. To remove one ship, tap it on your grid, then press Clear-Ship(C).

    Start: When all ships are placed, use Confirm in the Your Fleet control above the boards.

    Battle: Tap Enemy Waters to fire. Use the board switch on phones to inspect your fleet.

    Win: Sink every enemy ship before the enemy sinks yours.

    Review: After the match, press View Analysis to see move patterns and improvement tips.
